    Face Painting

    Have you got a event and want to add a fun activity for both children and adults?.

    Face Painting is a water activated safe way to paint on skin and can be removed with warm water and eye safe soap. Face painting at private events from Birthdays, Weddings to Corporate Functions and public Fairs, Fetes and Festivals. With designs from Animals to Superheroes, Flora to Boohoo and Tribal, supporting any theme or promotional colours and branding. Providing face paint as well as body paint designs. Also a range of neon paints to add that glow using ultra violet lighting. Using EU regulated products and FDA approved, skin friendly and cosmeticaly safe paint.

    Glitter Tattoos

    Would you like to bring sparkle to your event?.

    Glitter Tattoos are a temporary tattoo that is waterproof. We stock over 300 design choices with a large range of glitter colours. We can do themed designs from princess, pirates to sea life and jungle animals. Temporary Tattos can be removed using Baby oil. Using biodegradable, cruelty-free glitter made using eucalyptus plants.
